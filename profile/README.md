# Poignant Reminders

> Poignant Reminds is an asym game set in The Memory Isle, a purgatory for souls stripped of their memories by tragedy.

![Banner](Assets/Banner.png)

## Introduction: The Memory Island

Has something unfortunate ever changed your life? Something out of conventional, a 360 spin to your routine... Well, here, in **The Memory Isle**, those whoʼs lifes have been changed by *destinyʼs tragedy* get sent into here, *trapped* forever with *no memory of what once was or what they once were*.

The days in Memory Isle arenʼs quite a big thing, theyʼre silent and peaceful, but something horrible happens when the *night strikes*, when thereʼs no light in sight. Once the sun grants its place to the Moon, *they* walk around, leaving a trail of nothing more than despair... And whoever gets in *their way*? Oh, poor soul that wonʼt get to admire the beautiful stars... How pity.

### Islanders

Islanders are those who have been sent to Memory Island. They have little to no memories of their *“past life”*. Islanders work hard day and night, doing whatever it takes to *survive* and find an *exit*.

### Allnighters

Night hunters, those whoʼs job is to make the Islandersʼ goal *impossible* to reach. Blood-thirsty beings that will chase you down to *dead*, until no proof of your existence remains.

### The Awakening

Dawn, what Islanders like to call *“The Awakening”*, once the sun returns to its glorious pedestal, it seems like the night before was just a poor horror image. Islanders who were lost in battle will revive. But it only takes for the sun to vanish once again, for the nightmare to begin.

## Islander Classes

- **Dreamer**
As a class based in survival, dreamersʼ abilities are supposed to be helpful when it comes to hiding, running away, or just surviving the night. Whilst dreamers may not be helpful when it comes to team action, they can go on their own without any problem.

- **Watchman**
Those in charge of protection. With expectations on their shoulders and responsability on their back, watchmenʼs job is to ensure safety for all Islanders, even if itʼs risky.

- **Shaman**
Kind souls, healers of the group. Shamans make sure that their teammates are in a good physical state to continue the night, providing aid. Theyʼre quite reliable people, have a problem? They will surely help.

- **Back-up**
If you like supporting people, this class is for you! Back-upʼs job is to help the Islanders by enhacing their abilities, providing buffs to skills such as stamina, health and speed.

> More classes may be added.
